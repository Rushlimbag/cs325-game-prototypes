Golden Age v2.0

  • Originality:
  • Technical Merit: Players can move individual units using hotkeys while the board keeps track of positions. Territory can be captured through RNG battles with some bonuses tabulated with units.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Couldn't figure out how to guarantee a commander was on the border before attacking so he can attack from anywhere. Didn't have time to add in rulesets for other units so they just add a passive bonus to atk/def rolls when in an invaded space. Redesigned party board to store actual sprites as opposed to just numbers. Added a pause after every move that is unpaused with a click to prevent constant polling when mouse button is down (would cause multiple moves to play at once before).

  • Gameplay:
  • **I RECOMMEND OPENING THE CONSOLE (F12) FOR HELPFUL MESSAGES DURING PLAY**
  • **The game will pause itself after every turn and wait for the mouse to be clicked before a player can take their turn. Make sure you click once (whilst holding NO additional keys) before you make a move.**
    • Each player first picks a spot for their capital: red must place theirs in the top row while blue must place it in the bottom. Players then take turns claiming territory adjacent to already claimed land by simply clicking an empty square. Units can be moved by holding their associated hotkey and clicking the spot you wish to move them to (must be territory you hold for all units except commander/shield).
  • HOTKEYS:
    • King/Crown (k + click)
    • Queen/Eye (q + click)
    • Commander/Shield (c + click)
    • Assassin/Hood(a + click)
    Commanders can attack any enemy territory adjacent to a held territory by moving to that square. A dice roll will occur with outcomes posted to console. When defending, any units in the square being attacked provide a +1 to the defense roll.
