Golden Age v2.0

  • Originality:
  • Technical Merit: Players can click anywhere in a particular cell on the map and change data/the board. There is also adjacency checks internally to keep players from placing flags in cells not adjacent to others of the same type and automatic turn switching.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Checking for adjacency was very difficult until i figured out I could just add a a 'boundary' to my array.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Loading screen: Blue Gradient
    • Loading bar: edited from 'template-gamestate-functional'
    • All other assets were created by me
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
  • Analog Assignment: Golden Age
  • I referenced Ashley Crouch's Weird Checkers prototype for help creating my internal board structure: Weird Checkers
  • High Concept: 2 players fight for control of territories in a war between kingdoms.
  • Theme: N/A
  • Mandated Variety: N/A
  • Prototype Goal: Get a working board with clickable cells to allow addition of rulesets and more sprites. Really just trying to get core functionality done.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience a strategic game of war with simple rules and relatively quick playtime.
  • Gameplay: Each player first picks a spot for their capital: red must place theirs in the top row while blue must place it in the bottom. Players then take turns claiming territory adjacent to already claimed land. Eventually, I will implement unit types and a claim limit with mechanics for conquering enemy territories/capitals. But for now only basic functionality is implemented.

  • Story/Setting/Premise: The title is a reference to Berserk, hopefully you get the idea.
  • Target Audience: Strategy game fans.
  • Play Time: A few minutes.
